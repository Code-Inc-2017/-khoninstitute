About Us

#Khon Institute Orthopaedic Surgery Medical Practice cc

#Khon Institute is a general orthopaedic practice (i.e. bone, joint and muscle diseases) The practice derives its name (#Khon) from an indigenous Damara word translated to "Bones" in English.

sub-disciplines of #Khon Institute are:

  • surgery
  • Pediatric orthopedics
  • Hand surgery
  • Foot surgery
  • Reconstruction
  • Trauma


The #Khon Institute is wholly owned by a Namibian orthopaedic surgeon Dr. Stanley Ndakoro Kanyemba. Dr. Kanyemba was born and raised in Tsumeb and attended school at Nomtsoub primary before moving to Concordia College for his secondary education. Dr. Kanyemba went to the University of Namibia before embarking his journey into medical school.
